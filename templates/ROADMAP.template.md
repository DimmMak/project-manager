## 🔁 Promotion rules

- **Tier 4 → Tier 3:** pain genuinely hit at current scale
- **Tier 3 → Tier 2:** trigger condition met
- **Tier 2 → Tier 1:** usage data confirms value
- **Tier 1 → SHIPPED:** built, tested, CHANGELOG'd

Items can move BACK tiers (demoted) when:
- Built too early and problem never materialized
- Simpler solution emerged from a different direction
- Usage patterns reveal it's actually Tier 4 overkill

4. **Composition over complexity** — add small improvements, not new systems
5. **Observable reality** — every tier has a concrete measurable trigger
